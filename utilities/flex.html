Direction

Format:

.flex-direction-{value}

Values:

  • row
  • row-reverse
  • column
  • column-reverse

Example:

column 1
column 2
column 3 
<div class="display-flex flex-direction-row-reverse">
  <div class="padding-3 margin-3 background-primary">
    column 1
  </div>
  <div class="padding-3 margin-3 background-primary">
    column 2
  </div>
  <div class="padding-3 margin-3 background-primary">
    column 3
  </div>
</div>
